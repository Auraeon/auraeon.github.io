    • Meet Devin Gaughan.

    I am a computer programmer and test automation engineer based in Bangkok, Thailand, but I'm originally from Portland, Oregon, United States.

    My mission is to help usher humanity into a more fully automated future.
    My dream is that we can all spend more of the limited time we're given, to enjoy our lives.

    I have worked on teams at HP, Intel, and Xerox, among others.
    I have a passion for creating and building things that people can use to work smarter, not harder.

    I am currently working on a project that will help automate some of the most tedious tasks involved in machine learning algorithm training.
    This project is a passion of mine that I am building with a larger vision in mind. I hope to make tools that will not only change the way technology and science are performed, but are also stepping stones to a more utopian-esque society.
