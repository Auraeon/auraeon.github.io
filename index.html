About

Meet Devin Gaughan.


I am a programmer and developer based out of Portland, Oregon. I studied Computer Science in my hometown of Vancouver, Washington at Washington State University - Vancouver. I have worked on Firmware Engineering teams at HP, Intel, and Xerox. Most of my experience involves 2D and 3D printer technology. I have worked in hardware servicing with Fujifilm and Epson products. I experiment with a Prusa i3 3D printer at home to continually expand my knowledge of advancing print technologies.
